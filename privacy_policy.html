Privacy Policy — Inslider

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Inslider (“the app”, “we”) is a photo editor that runs entirely on your device. This policy explains what the app does with your data. Short version: we do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

What data we collect

None. Inslider has no account system, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. We do not operate any server that receives your data.

Photos and media

When you pick a photo, all editing (crop, blur, text, background blur) happens locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to us or any third party. Edited projects and settings are stored only in the app’s local storage on your device and are removed when you delete the project or the app.

On-device machine learning

The optional background blur feature uses Google ML Kit Selfie Segmentation, which runs fully on-device. Your images are processed locally and are never sent to Google or anyone else.

Purchases

Inslider offers an optional Pro upgrade. Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment details; Google handles the transaction and it is covered by Google’s own privacy policy.

Permissions

  • Photos / media — to let you pick a photo to edit and to save the result to your gallery. Used only when you choose to.

Children

Inslider does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Data retention & deletion

Because we do not collect any data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete on a server. To remove locally stored projects, delete them in the app (Settings → Delete all projects) or uninstall the app.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date will change accordingly.
